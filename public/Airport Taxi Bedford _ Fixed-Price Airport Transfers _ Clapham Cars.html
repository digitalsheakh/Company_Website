Airport transfers

Fed up of overpriced, unreliable taxis?

Forget trawling multi-storey car parks for a space, parking miles from the terminal and waiting forever for a shuttlebus, while paying a fortune for the privilege. Let us take the stress out of your next trip to the airport. Leave the car at home and travel by taxi instead.

Clapham Cars offers competitive and reliable airport transfers to all UK airports, including Luton Airport, all London Airports (Heathrow, Gatwick, Stansted and London City) and Birmingham Airport.

With our Meet & Greet service, one of our drivers will be waiting for you at the arrivals lounge when you return, ready to transport you home in comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an airport transfer from Bedford?

Booking an airport transfer from Bedford is quick and easy. You can book online, via our app, or by phone for a reliable, stress-free journey to any major UK airport, including Heathrow, Gatwick, Luton, Stansted, and Birmingham.

Do you offer fixed prices for airport transfers?

Yes! Our airport transfer service provides fixed-price fares, so you know the cost before you travel. No hidden fees—just a smooth, reliable ride to your destination.

What airports do you provide transfers to and from?

We offer airport transfers to and from all major UK airports, including:

  • Heathrow Airport
  • Gatwick Airport
  • Luton Airport
  • Stansted Airport
  • Birmingham Airport

Wherever you need to go, we’ve got you covered!

Can I book a last-minute airport transfer?

Yes! We operate 24/7, so you can book a last-minute airport transfer from Bedford whenever you need one. However, for the best availability, we recommend booking in advance.

Do you provide meet and greet services for airport transfers?

Yes, our airport transfer service includes a meet and greet option, where your driver will wait at arrivals with a sign, assist with luggage, and ensure a smooth journey to your next destination.
