Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

At Sheakh App Studio, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a trustworthy experience with our app.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Location data: Used solely for calculating accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Device settings: To save your app preferences

All data processing happens directly on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • Prayer Times: Location data is used to calculate accurate prayer times
  • Qibla Direction: Location is used to determine the direction of Kaaba
  • Preferences: To save your app settings

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information on external servers.

Notifications

If enabled, we send local notifications for prayer times. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and can be managed in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

We don't share any data with third parties. The app functions independently on your device.
